COMPOSER: ONE NIGHT GUESTS

One Night Guests is a 2024 Nigerian-Ghanaian Christmas comedy-thriller produced by Oldfilm Productions that premiered in cinemas on December 25, 2024. The music and soundtrack for it were composed by Sipumzo.

COMPOSER: SOWETO UPRISING - THE AFTERMATH

Later titled "Imivumba YamaQhawe" the production of the youth opera is one of the highlights of Sipumzo's career. Working with two schools in Khayelitsha, Chris Hand Arts and Culture School as well as Lathi-tha LSEN School of skills, our director was able to help bring life to stories from our past.

OCHESTRATION: COVER ME IN SUNSHINE

RMB Starlight Classics is a premier South African AfroSymphonic concert series, established in 1999. Held in Cape Town and Johannesburg, it features a diverse blend of classical, opera, jazz, and pop music performed by local and international artists. On this occasion, the iThemba Youth Choir, from Khayelitsha, performs Sipumzo's joyful orchestration, featuring vernacular lyrics and a heartfelt rap verse.

SOUNDTRACK COMPOSER: "1960"
